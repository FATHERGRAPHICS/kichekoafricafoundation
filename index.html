About Us

───────

Welcome to Kicheko Africa Foundation

KICHEKO AFRICA FOUNDATION established to collaborate with local and foreign organizations, agencies and institutions and various communities to provide education and enthusiasm in helping them, strengthening and raising the systems of supporting their initiatives Community including Women and children are facing challenges such as employee's harassment and embracement, unemployment, lack of education, ignorance, lack of help from the community, health challenges, influence of peer groups, issues of violence gender and poor access to technology development, desperation and inequality in financial resources that are an obstacle on utilizing their potential for community development.

────────────

Kicheko Africa Foundation

────────────

Vision

To be the leading provider of sustainable and innovative solutions in our industry. To have a productive society, that touches community lives and becomes a blessing, hope and comfort for others.

Mission

To be the number one organization in providing education and enthusiasm in helping Community, touching their lives and improving a good environment by teaching them to have a habit of self-reliance.

Objectives

1. To fight against gender harassment, humiliation and all forms of discrimination facing an employees in a work place.
2. Promote education to the community.
